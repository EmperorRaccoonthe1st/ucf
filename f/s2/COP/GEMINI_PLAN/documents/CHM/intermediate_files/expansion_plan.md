## Chapter 5: Solutions & Aqueous Reactions
### Content to Add
- **Rules (Must Memorize):**
  - **Solubility Guidelines:**
    - Always Soluble: $NO_3^-$, $C_2H_3O_2^-$, Group 1 ($Li^+, Na^+, K^+$), $NH_4^+$.

- **Expansion Instructions:**

## Chapter 6: Heat & Enthalpy
### Content to Add
- **Equations (Provided):**
  - Specific Heat: $q = m c_s \Delta T$
- **Equations (Must Memorize):**
  - Enthalpy from Formation: $\Delta H^\circ = \sum n\Delta H_f^\circ(\text{products}) - \sum n\Delta H_f^\circ(\text{reactants})$
  - Enthalpy from Bond Energies: $\Delta H^\circ = \sum (\text{bonds broken}) - \sum (\text{bonds formed})$
- **Expansion Instructions:**
  - Add $q = mc\Delta T$ to the **Calorimetry** section.
  - Group the two $\Delta H^\circ$ equations under **Reaction Enthalpy Calculations**. 
  - **Crucial Distinction:** Note that Formation uses (Products - Reactants) while Bond Energy uses (Reactants/Broken - Products/Formed).

## Chapter 8: States of Matter
### Content to Add
- **Constants (Provided):**

- **Expansion Instructions:**
  - Add $PV=nRT$ and constants to the **Ideal Gas Law** section.
  - Add Graham's Law to **Kinetic Molecular Theory** to explain effusion/diffusion.
  - Ensure the distinction between $R = 0.0821$ (Gases) and $R = 8.314$ (Energy, if applicable later) is clear.
