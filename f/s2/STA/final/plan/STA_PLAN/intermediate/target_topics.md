- **Question Number:** Q3b
- **Primary Concept:** Probability Rules

- **Primary Concept:** Conditional Probability

- **Question Number:** Q3d
- **Primary Concept:** Independence of Events

- **Source Dependency:** Chapter 4

- **Question Number:** Q3e
- **Primary Concept:** Multiplication Rule
- **Specific Skill:** Calculating probability of multiple events without replacement
- **Formula Needed:** $P(A \cap B \cap C) = P(A) \times P(B|A) \times P(C|A \cap B)$
- **Source Dependency:** Chapter 4

- **Question Number:** Q4a
- **Primary Concept:** Discrete Random Variables
- **Specific Skill:** Constructing a probability distribution table
- **Formula Needed:** $P(X=x) = n(x)/N$
- **Source Dependency:** Chapter 5

- **Question Number:** Q4b
- **Primary Concept:** Expected Value
- **Specific Skill:** Calculating the mean (expected value) of a discrete random variable
- **Formula Needed:** $E(X) = \sum [x \times P(x)]$
- **Source Dependency:** Chapter 5

- **Question Number:** Q4c
- **Primary Concept:** Variance and Standard Deviation
- **Specific Skill:** Calculating the standard deviation of a discrete random variable
- **Formula Needed:** $\sigma = \sqrt{\sum [x^2 \times P(x)] - \mu^2}$
- **Source Dependency:** Chapter 5

- **Question Number:** Q4d
- **Primary Concept:** Binomial Distribution
- **Specific Skill:** Calculating cumulative binomial probability $P(X \ge x)$
- **Formula Needed:** $P(X=x) = \binom{n}{x} p^x (1-p)^{n-x}$
- **Source Dependency:** Chapter 5

- **Question Number:** Q4e
- **Primary Concept:** Binomial Distribution Properties
- **Specific Skill:** Calculating mean and standard deviation of a binomial distribution
- **Formula Needed:** $\mu = n \times p$, $\sigma = \sqrt{n \times p \times (1-p)}$
- **Source Dependency:** Chapter 5

- **Question Number:** Q5a
- **Primary Concept:** Sampling Distribution of the Sample Mean
- **Specific Skill:** Finding the mean and standard error of the sample mean
- **Formula Needed:** $\mu_{\bar{x}} = \mu$, $\sigma_{\bar{x}} = \frac{\sigma}{\sqrt{n}}$
- **Source Dependency:** Chapter 6

- **Question Number:** Q5b
- **Primary Concept:** Central Limit Theorem
- **Specific Skill:** Calculating the probability of a sample mean falling within a range
- **Formula Needed:** $Z = \frac{\bar{x} - \mu_{\bar{x}}}{\sigma_{\bar{x}}}$
- **Source Dependency:** Chapter 6

- **Question Number:** Q5c
- **Primary Concept:** Sampling Distribution of Sample Proportion
- **Specific Skill:** Verifying the conditions for normal approximation (np and nq >= 10)
- **Formula Needed:** $n \times p \ge 10$, $n \times (1-p) \ge 10$
- **Source Dependency:** Chapter 7

- **Question Number:** Q5d
- **Primary Concept:** Sampling Distribution of Sample Proportion
- **Specific Skill:** Calculating the mean and standard error of the sample proportion
- **Formula Needed:** $\mu_{\hat{p}} = p$, $\sigma_{\hat{p}} = \sqrt{\frac{p(1-p)}{n}}$
- **Source Dependency:** Chapter 7

- **Question Number:** Q5e
- **Primary Concept:** Sampling Distribution of Sample Proportion
- **Specific Skill:** Calculating probabilities involving sample proportions
- **Formula Needed:** $Z = \frac{\hat{p} - \mu_{\hat{p}}}{\sigma_{\hat{p}}}$
- **Source Dependency:** Chapter 7
