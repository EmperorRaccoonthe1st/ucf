\section*{Question 6}
A university library committee wants to estimate the proportion of students who support extending library hours until 2:00 AM on weekdays. A random sample of $300$ students is selected, and $210$ students say they support the idea.

\textbf{Instruction:} Show all steps.

\begin{enumerate}[label=(\alph*)]
    \item Construct a $95\%$ confidence interval for the true proportion of all students who support extending library hours.
    \item Interpret the confidence interval in context.
\end{enumerate}
