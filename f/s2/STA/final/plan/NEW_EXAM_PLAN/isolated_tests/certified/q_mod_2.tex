\section*{Question 2}
A student organization records the weekly grocery spending, in dollars, for a group of $13$ students:
$40, 42, 45, 48, 50, 52, 55, 58, 62, 70, 85, 110, 145$

\textbf{Instruction:} Show all steps. Answers without supporting work will not receive full credit.

\begin{enumerate}[label=(\alph*)]
    \item Find the five-number summary:
    \begin{enumerate}[label=\roman*.]
        \item Minimum
        \item First quartile, $Q_1$
        \item Median, $Q_2$
        \item Third quartile, $Q_3$
        \item Maximum
    \end{enumerate}

    \item Construct a boxplot of the data. Clearly label:
    \begin{itemize}
        \item $Q_1$, median, and $Q_3$
        \item Whiskers
        \item Any outliers
    \end{itemize}

    \item Based on the boxplot, describe the shape of the distribution as symmetric, left-skewed, or right-skewed. Justify your answer.
\end{enumerate}
