\section*{Question 5}
The average weekly study time of students in a large university population is $\mu = 15$ hours with a standard deviation of $\sigma = 4$ hours. A random sample of size $n = 49$ students is selected.

\textbf{Instruction:} Show all steps. Answers without supporting work may not receive full credit.

\begin{enumerate}[label=(\alph*)]
    \item Find the mean and standard deviation of the sampling distribution of the sample mean $\bar{X}$.
    \item Find the probability that the sample mean study time is between $14$ and $16$ hours.
\end{enumerate}

Now consider a different scenario:
It is known that $40\%$ of students at this university use AI tools for their coursework regularly. A random sample of size $n = 150$ students is selected. Let $\hat{p}$ be the sample proportion of students who use AI tools regularly.

\begin{enumerate}[label=(\alph*), resume]
    \item Verify whether the normal approximation can be used for $\hat{p}$.
    \item Find the mean and standard deviation of the sampling distribution of $\hat{p}$.

\end{enumerate}
