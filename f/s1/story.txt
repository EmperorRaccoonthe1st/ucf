I had been at SCAD for about a two months when I finally decided that I needed a break.
My super awesome and cute long distance boyfriend was missing me for what like seemed like forever.
I decided that it would do me some good to visit him in Orlando.
Everyone knows how dreary SCAD can get if you let the school bring you really affect your mental state.

The weekend had been wonderful, just what I had needed.
I didn't even realize how much I had missed the boy until I found myself wrapped up in his arms.
The four and half hour drive was so worth it, just to see his face in person.

It was a weekend in heaven, except that in all my effort to leave my school behind it had still followed me there.
The thing about SCAD is that its all just projects, projects, projects.
It isn't a normal college like UCF, they don't have normal tests or assignments.
In my bid for a precious couple of days to relax I had forgotten all about my projects that were due.

These projects of mine were no joke, the kind of thing that takes a weekend of round the clock work just to barely scrape out a passing grade.
I hadn't ever put my art skill to the test like that before.
I had to draw an anlitike the size of a large poster, in blistering detail.
