# Intro To Philosophy
The Threat of Randomness

## Randomness Argument

    R1: If determinism is false, then it is a matter of random chance that I chose not to lie.

    R2: I have no control over what happens as a matter of random chance.

    R3: Therefore, if determinism is false then I have no control over the fact that I chose not to lie.

    Defense of R1:
        - The Rollback Intuition:
            If the world would be rolled back repeatedly then the probability of events occurring would even out into near 50/50's belying the "choice" was really the result of mere probability.

    Defense of R2:
        If the chance is truly random then there is practically no choice and therefore no control.

## Two Threats to Free Will

    - Threat 1: Determination

    - Threat 2: Randomness
